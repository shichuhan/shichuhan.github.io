石楚涵 Chuhan SHI

I received my Ph.D. degree at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). I was cosupervised by Prof. Qiong Luo and Prof. Xiaojuan Ma. Before joining HKUST, I obtained my Bachelor degree from Shandong University in 2019. My main area of research is Human-AI Collaboration, Data Visualization, and Visual Analytics. My current research focuses on: 1) interactive AI for interdisciplinary studies (e.g., natural science, data science, medicine), and 2) LLM-powered visualization design.

I will join the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Southeast University as a tenure-track associate professor soon! We welcome undergraduate/master students and visiting interns. If you are interested in human-AI collaboration, data visualization, and visual analytics, please email me.
